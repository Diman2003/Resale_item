About Us

We are a hyperlocal platform to connect the waste producers to the waste collectors. Individuals, households, organisations, and dealers like Kabadiwalas, collectors, and recyclers all come together on a unified online platform. Here, a customer can easily find their nearest Kabadiwala and place an order for doorstep waste pickup. By leveraging AI, IoT, and IT in the waste management sector, We aim to help small local Kabadiwalas grow their businesses with the help of technology. By offering a modern solution to treat & manage waste, we enable our partners to collect waste efficiently with fewer resources.
